About The Scientist:


Ashlee Junier is a PhD candidate at Tufts University. Her research focuses on understanding the pathways and gene expression involved in Candida Albicans invasive filamentation. She studies in the Kumamoto Lab, under the supervision of Carol Kumamoto.


A girl scout, President of the Tufts Biomedical Bussiness Club (TBBC), member of the Tufts Graduate Student Council (GSC), and Graduate Women In Science and Engineering (GWISE), Ashlee is very involved in her community and interests.


In her free time she even comes out to work with Emerson students!
